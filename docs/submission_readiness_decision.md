# Submission Readiness Decision

Decision: workshop-only / strong-revise.

## Rationale

The paper has a clear diagnostic, reproducible toy evidence, and a v2 stress that prevents the censored main result from being overread. It is not full-submission ready without real deployment data and statistical treatment of censored/nonmonotonic skill curves.

## Allowed Claim

Robot skill reports should disclose temporal decay under a named drift process, including threshold, observation window, censoring, and preferably the full curve.

## Disallowed Claim

Sentinel-triggered rehearsal gives indefinite half-life. The v2 120-day stress shows it crosses the half-success threshold at 89.0 days.

## Next Work Needed For Full Submission

- Real robot deployment decay curves.
- Survival-analysis estimates with uncertainty.
- Multiple task families and drift processes.
- Evaluation cost and sampling protocol.
- Nonmonotonic recovery and catastrophic shock handling.
